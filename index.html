My Story So Far

My name's Samatar, 27, and I'm aiming to become a Front-End Developer in the next 12 months.

I was born and raised in London, and my parents are from Somalia. I joined P2T, because I want to gain skills that will allow me to move to a muslim country in the future.

I originally signed up to P2T in July 2024, but due to a combination of personal circumstances, and a lot of procrastinating, I put things to the side for a while. However, I decided to stop making excuses and start again. Better late than never as they say.

As someone who doesn't have a university degree, I'm inspired by people that didn't let that derail them and built a future for themselves, and I want to do the same by breaking into tech.

3 reasons why I want to be a developer:

  • Stable career and high income.
  • Gain skills and develop myself.
  • Flexibility and remote work, more freedom to do what I want.
